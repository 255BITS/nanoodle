Standing rules (from `shares.md` plan of record):
- **Cookoff/crypto framing ONLY in Nano venues.** Everywhere else: single-file .html
  export + no-server/no-analytics + BYO-key. Never lead with "win crypto" outside Nano.

- **Showcase user-built noodles over your own** wherever possible — winners already
  agreed to credited featuring (contest rule), and "look what someone made with it"
  converts better than "look what I made".

  first-hour comment replies.

  sit in the comments would burn the two best cards. Post-cookoff they're stronger —
  user-built winners are the social proof.
- AlternativeTo is hard-gated until Jul 13 05:49 Stockholm (7-day account age), which
  happens to be the ideal slot anyway.
